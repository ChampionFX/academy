How do I register to become a ChampionFX IB or affiliate?

Thank you for your interest in partnering with us. We’ve made it easy for you to apply online. Just fill out the online application form with all the necessary details. We will contact you directly if your application is successful.

How much do I get paid if I refer clients onto your Champion Trader platform?

Your earnings are based on our tiered and laddered commission rates:

Tier Total net revenue per month (USD) Commission Rates
1 $0 - 10,000 20%
2 10,001 - 50,000 25%
3 50,000 - 100,000 30%
4 100,001 + 35%

How much do I get paid if I refer clients onto your MT5 platform?

Your earnings are based on the trading done on each referred client’s account:

Account type Commission rates
Cent USD 8/lot
Standard USD 5/lot
STP USD 1.5/lot

When are my commissions paid?

Affiliate earnings from your Champion Trader referrals are paid out by the 15th of every month.

IB earnings from your MT5 referrals are credited instantly into your MT5 account.

Do you offer any bonus incentives for your IBs?

Yes, we do. At ChampionFX, we reward our IBs with extra incentives to recognise their hard work. Get your clients to trade more and we will pay more. It’s that simple.

This is how our IB bonus incentive scheme works:

Total lots traded in a month Bonus on monthly total earnings
300 – 499 lots 3%
500 – 999 lots 5%
Over 1,000 lots 8%

You bonus is activated when your clients hit a minimum total of lots traded in a month. It is then calculated based on your monthly total earnings from MT5 rebates.

For e.g. Your clients collectively traded a total of 301 lots last month, earning you a total of USD 1,000 in MT5 rebates. This means that you will receive a 3% bonus on top of your total earnings of USD 1,000 –– which amounts to USD 30.

How can I withdraw my commissions?

Affiliate commissions are credited into your ChampionFX real account by the 15th of every month. You can then withdraw your funds using any of the available withdrawal methods offered to ChampionFX real account holders.

IB commissions are credited directly into your MT5 account. You can transfer funds from your MT5 account to your ChampionFX real account, then withdraw the funds using any of the available withdrawal methods offered to ChampionFX real account holders.

Do you offer any promotional or marketing materials for your affiliates and IBs?

Yes, we will provide you with banners, links, reviews, newsletters, videos, and text ads that you can use to drive referrals to the ChampionFX website.

Do you offer any regional representative partnership opportunities?

Yes, we do. Please contact us at partnerships@champion-fx.com to find out more about how you can become a ChampionFX representative in your region.

Are there any minimum client or volume conditions that I need to meet before I can withdraw my commissions?

No, there are no minimum requirements to withdraw your affiliate or IB commissions.
