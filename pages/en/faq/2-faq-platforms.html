What are the major differences between the MT5 desktop app, web terminal, and mobile apps?

The MT5 desktop app and web terminal are designed for the large screens offered by desktop or laptop computers. The desktop app needs to be downloaded and installed on your device while the web terminal can be accessed from any web browser. The MT5 mobile apps were designed for optimal viewing on smaller devices without sacrificing the main functionalities of MT5.

Why can’t I purchase a ‘New Order’ on MT5?

These are the likely reasons why the ‘New Order’ button in your MT5 terminal is not available:

  • You are not connected to the Internet. Please check your Internet connection and try signing into the terminal again.
  • You signed into your account using your investor password, which has read-only permission. The ‘Journal’ tab at the bottom of the terminal will show [Investor Mode] if this is true.

How do I log into my MT5 platform?

Click on the ‘File’ menu tab located at the top left hand corner of the terminal. Then select ‘Login to Trade Account’ and the login popup window will appear. Enter your login and password, and make sure that the server that’s been assigned to you is correct.

How do I test Expert Advisors and trading signals before purchase?

You can test and evaluate the performance of trading robots using MT5’s built-in Strategy Tester before you purchase them. Simply download the trading robot directly into your MT5 terminal, then browse to the ‘Navigator’ window and click on the plus sign located next to ‘Expert Advisors’ to see all the applications that you’ve purchased or downloaded.

Look for the name of the trading robot you just downloaded, right click on it, and select ‘Test’ from the menu that appears to open the Strategy Tester panel. Set the parameters for the test according to your chosen testing method.

Is there a way for me to see my account balance and trading history in MT5?

You can find your account balance and trading history in the ‘Terminal’ (or ToolBox) window located at the bottom of the screen.

There are three tabs that are relevant to you in the ‘Terminal’ window:

  • Trade – Displays your account balance, open positions, and more
  • History – Shows you all the orders that you’ve placed on MT5
  • Journal – Shows you a list of actions

Can I export my trading history from MT5?

Yes, you can export your trading history by going to the ‘Report’ tab in the ‘Terminal’ window located at the bottom of the screen.

What is copy trading?

Copy trading is an innovative feature that allows you to subscribe to ‘trading signals’ that are provided by more experienced brokers so that you may copy their trades.

It also comes with multiple built-in tools for technical and fundamental analysis, plus support for trading robots and copy trading through the MetaTrader Market. How can I subscribe to trading signals and how much does it cost?

The trading signals service is a great way to learn trading by copying the operations of a more experienced trader. Head over to MT5’s trading signals showcase section to review and choose a signal. Trading signals can be free or for a fee. Paid signals start from USD 20 onwards.

Once you’ve subscribed to a signal, you’ll need to set up the copying parameters. Make sure that the deposit amount, spread value, and minimum deposit level are as you intended before you start copying.

How do I become a signal provider?

Have you come up with a unique and successful strategy? Then you can register as a trading signal service provider with MetaQuotes and earn a fixed fee from each subscriber. Please note that you’ll need to provide your contact details and copies of your personal identification.

After registration, you’ll need to specify the account that will be used as the trading signal source, create a name for your signal, and set a price. Once it’s been verified, your signal will be added to the trading signals showcase section where it will be available for subscription.

How can I install Expert Advisors and technical indicators on MT5?

Installing trading robots and technical indicators is easy and you can do it all without having to leave your MT5 platform.

First, go to the ‘Market’ tab in the ‘Toolbox’ window located at the bottom of your screen. In the top panel of the ‘Toolbox’ you can browse products by category, conveniently labelled on the individual tabs. After you’ve chosen a product, click on the ‘Buy’ button to purchase. The product is then automatically downloaded to your platform.

By the way, all the products you purchased are displayed in the ‘Purchased’ tab of the ‘Toolbox’. It also shows you if there are updated versions of the trading robot or technical indicator that you purchased.

Can I install Expert Advisors (EAs) without using the MetaTrader Market?

To add EAs from other sources, or even the ones you developed yourself, just follow these steps:

  1. Close the MT5 desktop application.
  2. Download and save y to your computer.
  3. Go to the following folder on your computer: C://Program Files/Champion Group/MQL5/Experts.
  4. Copy and paste your EA file into this folder.
  5. Restart the MT5 desktop application.
  6. To see the EA you’ve just installed, browse to the following menu options in MT5: View > Navigator > Expert Advisors.
  7. Drag and drop the EA from the Navigator window to the Forex chart you’d like to attach it to. Wait for a pop-up window to appear.
  8. Check ‘Allow live trading’ under the Common tab, then click OK.
  9. Click on the ‘Auto Trading’ button on the top menu to enable auto trading.

As a signal subscriber, do I need to have my MT5 terminal open in order to copy and execute trades?

Not at all. MT5’s virtual hosting feature lets you trade even when your device is turned off. If you’re using trading robots or trading signals and require uninterrupted operation, you can rent a virtual server directly from your MT5 platform to execute your trades at any time.
