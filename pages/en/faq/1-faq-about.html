Who is ChampionFX?

ChampionFX is a licensed broker with an unwavering commitment to client satisfaction, officially launched in 2017.

Are you licensed?

Yes, ChampionFX is licensed and regulated by the Vanuatu Financial Services Commission (VFSC).

ChampionFX is also registered with the Financial Intelligence Unit (FIU) of Vanuatu in compliance with VFSC Anti-Money Laundering rules.

What kind of products do you offer?

ChampionFX offers Forex, CFDs, futures, metals, and more on its MT5 platform, and binary options on its Champion Trader platform.

Where are you located?
