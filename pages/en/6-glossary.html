Spread

The spread is the difference between the bid (sell) and ask (buy) price. There are two types of spreads: floating and fixed.

  • Floating spread

    A floating spread is a constantly changing spread value, defined by real-world events and market volatility.

  • Fixed spread

    A fixed spread is a spread value that stays the same, no matter the time or market volatility.

Stops level

When you’ve reached the Stops level, you will not be able to place Stop Loss, Take Profit, and pending orders. If you do place an order, the server will return an ‘Invalid Stops’ message and your order will not be accepted.

T

Tick size

Tick size refers to the minimum price movement of the contract.

Tick value

Tick value is the cost of each minimum price movement of the contract.

Trade

The trading mode for the contract. Some of the common modes include full access, long only, and short only. Trading can also be completely prohibited.
