Gain access to the most popular trading tools to monitor, analyse, and forecast your favourite assets – all on MT5.

Technical analysis

Technical analysis is a way to forecast the price movement of a financial asset. You will study financial charts with the help of tools such as technical indicators and analytical objects.

In MT5, you can choose from a comprehensive set of tools:

  • 38 technical indicators
  • 44 analytical objects
  • 21 timeframes
  • One-minute history
  • Unlimited number of charts

You can also download thousands of third-party tools –– both free and paid –– from the MetaTrader Market or Code Base if you want to enhance your analysis even further.

Fundamental analysis

Fundamental analysis is another way to forecast price movement. You will study news events and macroeconomic indicators to see how they will impact the markets and your opportunities.

In MT5, you can access two built-in tools for a comprehensive fundamental analysis:

  • Financial News brings you the latest news from international news agencies, as well as newsletters – directly in the platform.
  • Economic Calendar publishes macroeconomic indicators that show you growth and decline in key economic benchmarks so you can forecast price trends. Macroeconomic indicators can even be displayed on the charts of corresponding currency pairs to give you a visual overview of how certain events are influencing currency movements.
